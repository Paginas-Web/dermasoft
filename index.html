Que es CXR?

CXR (CarbonXtremeROM) es una ROM optimizada con adaptaciones de CM, AOSP y un pequeño toque de PAC-ROM.

Historia de ROM-PacMe

CXR born how a free project between 4 guys interested in Android, we are a small group of guys, with basic knowledge of Android, currently still working to bring a new ROM to the world of Android, bringing more stability, support those devices that do not have with support from other developers

We had our first supported devices, Galaxy Ace i (cooperve) Galaxy Ace (cooper), etc. We currently have over current devices, Moto E, G Moto, Moto G 2014, S3, etc...
